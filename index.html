The Mandarin Palace

Mandarin Palace Takeaway is a Chinese takeaway restaurant established in 1989, serving the Northwest community for over three
decades by offering various traditional and modern Chinese dishes. Located in north-western Northern Ireland, Mandarin Palace Takeaway
prides itself on high-quality ingredients, authentic flavours, and affordable prices.

Whether you prefer the comfort of your own home or a quick stop for food collection, we've got you covered.
Treat yourself to our extensive menu of delicious Chinese dishes and experience the convenience of our takeaway.
