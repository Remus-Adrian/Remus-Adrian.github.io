Mandarin Palace Takeaway is a Chinese takeaway restaurant established in 1989, serving the Northwest community for over three decades by offering various traditional and modern dishes.
   Located in north-western Northern Ireland,Mandarin Takeaway prides itself on its high-quality ingredients, authentic flavours, and affordable prices.Additionally, it offers a seamless
      ordering experience through phone or online service, allowing customers to indulge in various culinary treasures. From savoury shumai and chicken dumplings to the delightful ,
       duck,chow Mein, and an array of tantalising options, there's something to please every palate. The restaurant also takes great care to accommodate specific dietary
           needs, with vegetarian and gluten-free alternatives available. With its reputation for warm and efficient service, Mandarin Palace ensures that customers
                          can relish their meals swiftly and effortlessly,whether it's a cosy evening at home or a joyous occasion with loved ones.
